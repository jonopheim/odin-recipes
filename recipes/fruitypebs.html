Fruity Pebbles & Milk

A Sweet Delight to Start Your Morning Right

This rice based cereal is a perfect balance of carbs and flavor. If you're gluten free go ahead and try some too!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Fruity Pebbles Cereal
  • 1/2-1 Cup Whole Milk

Directions

  1. Grab standard bowl out of kitchen cupboard.
  2. Pour in enough Fruity Pebbles to fill half the bowl.
  3. Slowly cover with whole milk straight from the container. Be careful to not pour too much as the cereal may get soggy.
  4. Enjoy slowly for best results.
