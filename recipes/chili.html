Aldi Chili

Chili's good any day of the week, but it's even better on those cold winter days

This is not a spicy chili. The seasoning from Aldi gives it quite enough flavor though!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef/Turkey
  • 1 14oz can diced tomatoes
  • 1 16oz can kidney beans
  • 1 16oz can sweet corn
  • 1 packet Stonemill Chili mix from Aldi

Directions

  1. Brown ground beef/turkey in a large pot. Season well with salt, pepper, and garlic powder while browning.
  2. Stir in cans of tomatoes, kidney beans, and corn. DO NOT drain!
  3. Mix in chili powder slowly so as not to spill.
  4. Set to medium heat and let cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Don't forget all the fixings like: sour cream, cheese, crackers, and hot sauce!
